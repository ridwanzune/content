reMind is an app that helps you remember important tasks and deadlines

Problem
Nowadays lots of people suffer from being late with their tasks, bad sleep routine and not accomplishing their goals. All that comes from a bad time management that isn't developed easily and requires a strong will, desire and patience

Solution
reMind solves the issue of time management thanks to the vast number of reminder types that solve a majority of people's issues. In addition to that you can create a team and assign or receive tasks and reminders from your family members, students and colleagues

Rachel is a 17 year old high school student who is always forgetting something

David is a 29 year old designer who decided to become a mentor

Motivation
Rachel always struggles with time management and the basic methods of solving this don't help her. That's why she decided to find herself an app that will help her remind her about various important tasks

Wants and needs
• Wants the app to have different types of reminders
• Wants to find reminders fast
• Wants reminders to be filtered
• Wants to have her reminders grouped
• Wants to cooperate with other people through the app
